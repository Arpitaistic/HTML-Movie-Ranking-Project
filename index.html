The Best Movies According To Arpita

3 All Time Best Movies

Divergent

"Divergent" is a dystopian science fiction film that offers a captivating premise but falls short in execution. While its intriguing concept of a society divided into distinct factions based on personality traits initially engages viewers, the film struggles with pacing issues and character development. Despite some strong performances, the plot's predictability and lack of depth prevent it from fully realizing its potential. Overall, "Divergent" delivers a visually interesting world but fails to fully immerse audiences in its narrative.

Me Before You

"Me Before You" is a poignant and emotionally charged romantic drama. The film skillfully explores the relationship between a caregiver and a quadriplegic man, delving into themes of love, choice, and the value of life. With strong performances from its lead actors, the movie strikes a balance between heartwarming moments and the somber realities of disability. While it has been criticized for its portrayal of disability issues, it sparks thought-provoking discussions about individual agency and personal growth. "Me Before You" is a beautifully acted and emotionally resonant film that leaves a lasting impact on its audience.

The Mortal Instruments: City of Bones

"The Mortal Instruments: City of Bones" is a film adaptation of the first book in Cassandra Clare's "The Mortal Instruments" series. Released in 2013, the movie aimed to bring the supernatural urban fantasy world to the big screen. While it stayed faithful to certain elements of the source material and had a visually appealing fantasy setting, the film faced criticism for its pacing, character development, and deviations from the book's plot. Despite efforts to launch a film franchise, the movie's mixed reception and box office performance led to the cancellation of planned sequels. Overall, "The Mortal Instruments: City of Bones" has its merits for fans of the book series but falls short of fully capturing the essence of the source material.
